For Our Community

Member Services

Supporting the health, education, housing, and well-being of every Lummi citizen.

Health Center

Comprehensive medical, dental, and pharmacy services for enrolled members and eligible community members.

Hours:
Medical: M-F 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Dental: M-F 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM
Pharmacy: M-F 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM

Visit lummihealth.gov →

Education Services

K-12 support, scholarships, higher education assistance, cultural education programs, and early learning resources.

Investing in the education of our youth from early childhood through college and career.

Housing Authority

Low-income rental housing, rental assistance, home repair programs, supportive housing, and homeownership support.

Hours: M-F 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM

Visit lummihousing.com →

Enrollment Services

Tribal enrollment applications, enrollment verification, descendancy documentation, and genealogy support.

Phone: (360) 312-2398
Email: lummienrollment@lummi-nsn.gov
Hours: M-F 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM

Workforce Development

Job training, career counseling, employment placement assistance, and skills development programs.

Phone: (360) 312-2063
Director: Vanessa Cooper
Hours: M-F 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM

Food Distribution

USDA food programs, emergency food assistance, and nutrition support for eligible tribal members.

Phone: (360) 380-6957
Hours: M-F 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM

Counseling Services

Mental health support, substance abuse counseling, family services, and culturally-informed care.

Confidential support for individuals and families. Contact Health Center for referrals.

Cultural Programs

Language preservation, traditional arts, cultural education, and community events celebrating Lhaq'temish heritage.

Supporting cultural continuity and language revitalization for future generations.
