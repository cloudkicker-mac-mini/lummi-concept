News & Announcements

Stay informed about community updates, programs, and important notices from Lummi Nation.

Program Update February 20, 2026

SWMS Program Update

The Solid Waste Management System (SWMS) program is implementing new recycling initiatives to improve environmental sustainability on the reservation. New drop-off locations will be available starting March 1st.

For more information, contact the SWMS office at (360) 312-2000.

Natural Resources February 15, 2026

Salmon Need Water Campaign Continues

The Lummi Nation's Salmon Need Water initiative continues to advocate for water flow restoration in critical salmon habitat. Recent agreements with regional partners are showing promising results for salmon recovery.

Learn More at salmonneedwater.org →
IT Training February 10, 2026

Microsoft 365 Training Available

The IT Help Desk is offering Microsoft 365 training sessions for all tribal employees. Sessions cover Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive, and other productivity tools. Contact the Help Desk to schedule training.

Contact: (360) 312-2036 | helpdesk@lummi-nsn.gov

Directory February 5, 2026

Updated Tribal Phone Directory Available

The 2026 Lummi Nation phone directory is now available online and at the main office. Updated contact information for all departments and key personnel is included.

Housing Notice February 11, 2026

Housing Authority: 30-Day Notice Requirement

Effective immediately, all rental housing tenants must provide a minimum 30-day written notice prior to moving out. Notices should be submitted to the Housing Authority office during business hours.

Housing Authority Hours: M-F 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM | lummihousing.com
