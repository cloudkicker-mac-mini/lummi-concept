Wellness & Movement

Fitness Center

Supporting the physical wellness of our community through accessible fitness resources and programs.

The Lummi Tribal Health Center Fitness Center offers facilities and programs designed to promote healthy, active lifestyles for the community.

Personal Training

One-on-One Support

The Fitness Center is staffed with trained personnel available to assist with equipment orientation, exercise guidance, and personalized fitness planning.

Group Fitness

Classes & Programs

Group fitness classes provide structured workout opportunities in a supportive community setting. Classes are designed for various fitness levels and interests.

Challenges & Partnerships

Stay Motivated

Seasonal fitness challenges and community partnerships create accountability and fun. Participants work toward health goals alongside fellow community members.

Ready to Get Started?

Contact the Health Center for fitness center hours, membership, and current class schedules.
