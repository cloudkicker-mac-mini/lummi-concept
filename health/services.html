Comprehensive Care

Healthcare Services

From primary care to specialized treatment, we provide a full range of medical, dental, behavioral health, and wellness services.

Adult Medical Care

Comprehensive primary care for adults including chronic disease management, preventative services, and routine care.

  • Primary Care
  • Imaging
  • Laboratory Services
  • Chronic Disease Management
  • Diabetes Education
  • Nutrition Services
  • Home Visits
  • Medications for Opioid Use Disorder

Behavioral Health

Mental health therapy services for individuals, couples, and families.

  • Individual Therapy
  • Couples Therapy
  • Family Mental Health Therapy

Dental

Complete dental care from preventative cleanings to advanced restorative procedures.

  • Preventative Dentistry
  • Restorative Dentistry
  • Dental Implants
  • Dentures & Partials
  • Emergency Dental Care
  • Endodontics (Root Canals)
  • Oral Surgery
  • Orthodontics
  • Sedation Dentistry

Harm Reduction & Hepatitis C

Services to reduce harm and support community health and safety.

  • Chronic Hepatitis C Elimination
  • Narcan Safety Programs
  • Syringe Service Program

Maternal Medicine

Specialized care for expectant mothers and maternal health services.

Pediatric Care

Primary care and preventative services for children and adolescents.

  • Primary Care
  • Immunizations
  • School Based Health Program
  • WIC Services

Pharmacy

Full-service pharmacy providing prescription medications for eligible direct care patients with expert pharmacist consultation.

Hours: M-F 8:30AM - 5PM

Physical Therapy

Rehabilitation and therapeutic services to restore mobility, strength, and function.

Psychiatry

Psychiatric evaluation and medication management for mental health conditions.

Public Health

Community health initiatives and infectious disease prevention.

  • STI Testing
  • COVID-19 & Respiratory Viruses
  • Opioid/Fentanyl Response

Purchase & Referred Care (PRC)

Coverage for approved healthcare services not provided at the Health Center.

  • Specialty Referral Coverage
  • Vision & Hardware
  • Health Benefits Sign-up
  • Billing Services

Note: PRC is the Payor of Last Resort. Alternate insurance must be pursued first.

Rheumatology

Diagnosis and treatment of autoimmune and inflammatory conditions affecting joints, muscles, and bones.

Social Work

Support services connecting patients with resources, assistance programs, and community support.

Transportation

Transportation assistance for eligible patients to access medical appointments and healthcare services.

Ready to Get Started?

New patients can register online or by calling our office. Existing patients can schedule appointments through MyChart or by phone.
