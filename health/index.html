Comprehensive Care for Our Community

The Lummi Tribal Health Center provides medical, dental, pharmacy, behavioral health, and wellness services to the Lummi people and eligible Native Americans. Our mission is to raise the health status of our community to the highest possible level.

What We Offer

Healthcare Services

Adult Medical Care

Primary care, chronic disease management, diabetes education, imaging, and laboratory services.

Dental

Preventative, restorative, emergency care, implants, orthodontics, and sedation dentistry.

Pharmacy

Prescription medications for eligible direct care patients with expert pharmacist support.

Behavioral Health

Individual, couples, and family mental health therapy services.

Pediatric Care

Primary care, immunizations, school-based health program, and WIC services.

Physical Therapy

Rehabilitation and therapeutic services to support mobility and wellness.

Walk-In Welcome

Same-Day Appointments Available

We understand that health concerns don't always wait. The Lummi Tribal Health Center offers same-day appointments for urgent medical needs. Call us at (360) 384-0464 to schedule or check in at patient registration.

Community Health

Serving Our People

Healthcare rooted in community values. We serve the Lummi people and eligible Native Americans with culturally sensitive care that honors our traditions while providing modern medical excellence.
