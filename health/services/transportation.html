Transportation

Getting patients to and from their health appointments.

The Transportation program provides rides to and from health appointments for eligible patients. This service helps ensure that transportation barriers do not prevent community members from accessing the care they need at the Lummi Tribal Health Center and approved outside facilities.

To schedule a ride: Contact the Health Center at (360) 384-0464 to arrange transportation for your appointment.
