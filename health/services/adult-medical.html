Adult Medical Care

Our adult medical services provide comprehensive primary care and specialized treatment for adults. Our dedicated team of providers is committed to raising the health status of the Lummi people and other American Indians and Alaskan Natives to the highest possible level.

Hours

Monday – Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Phone: (360) 384-0464

Our Services

Primary Care

Preventive care, health screenings, and treatment for acute and chronic conditions.

Imaging

Diagnostic imaging services to support accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

Laboratory

On-site lab services for blood work, urinalysis, and other diagnostic testing.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care for diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and other chronic conditions.

Diabetes Education

Specialized education and support for diabetes prevention and management.

Nutrition

Nutritional counseling and meal planning to support overall health and wellness.

Home Visits

In-home medical care for patients who are unable to travel to the clinic.

Medications for Opioid Use Disorder

Evidence-based medication treatment for opioid use disorder in a supportive environment.
