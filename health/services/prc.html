Services

Purchase & Referred Care

Helping eligible patients access specialty care outside the Health Center.

The Purchased and Referred Care (PRC) program assists eligible patients with payment for health care services provided at outside facilities. These services supplement, but do not duplicate, direct services available at the Lummi Tribal Health Center. Federal regulations require the Payor of Last Resort ruling — individuals must apply for alternate insurance (Medicaid, Medicare, state programs) to be eligible.

Specialty Referral Coverage

Coverage for approved specialist visits and procedures at external facilities.

Vision & Hardware

Vision exams and eyewear coverage for eligible patients.

Health Benefits Sign-up

Assistance enrolling in Medicaid, Medicare, and other state health insurance programs.

Billing

Billing questions and insurance coordination for services received.
