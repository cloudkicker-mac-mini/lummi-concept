Employment Opportunities

Lummi Nation is one of the largest employers in Whatcom County, with diverse career opportunities across dozens of departments.

Why Work at Lummi Nation?

Competitive Benefits

Comprehensive benefits package including health, dental, retirement, and paid time off.

Serve Your Community

Make a meaningful impact serving Lummi citizens and the broader tribal community.

Career Growth

Professional development opportunities and pathways for advancement across departments.

How to Apply

Application Process

  1. Download and complete the official LIBC employment application (PDF)
  2. Attach all required supporting documents (resume, cover letter, certifications, references)
  3. If you are enrolled in a Federally Recognized Tribe, attach a copy of your enrollment card to qualify for Native Preference Policy
  4. Submit your completed application packet by 4:30 PM on the closing date of the position posting

Native Preference Policy

The Lummi Indian Business Council is committed to providing employment preference to qualified Native American candidates in accordance with federal law and tribal policy.

If you are enrolled in a Federally Recognized Tribe, please include a copy of your enrollment card with your application to receive preference consideration.

Equal Opportunity Employer

Lummi Nation is an Equal Opportunity Employer committed to providing a workplace free from discrimination and harassment. All qualified applicants receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability status, protected veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law, except as permitted by the Native American preference laws.
