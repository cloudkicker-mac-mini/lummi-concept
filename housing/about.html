About LNHA

Supporting tribal members through housing since 2006

Our Mission

Increasing Housing Opportunities

"Support tribal members by increasing opportunities for housing that is decent, safe, sanitary, and affordable, and to promote opportunities for homeownership."

Our History

Serving Since 2006

The Lummi Nation Housing Authority was created in 2006 by Lummi Indian Business Council (LIBC).

Since our founding, we have developed over 350 low-income rental and homeownership units across the Lummi Reservation.
