Forms & Applications

Download the forms you need for housing programs and services

All forms are in PDF format. After completing your application, you can submit it in person, by mail, or contact our office at (360) 312-8407 for assistance.

Housing Applications

Housing Application

General housing application for rental housing, homeownership programs, and down payment assistance. Required for all LNHA housing programs.

Used for: Low-Income Rental, Down Payment Assistance, New Construction
Download PDF →

Waiting List Update

Annual requirement — All applicants on the rental housing waiting list must complete and submit this form annually to remain on the waiting list.

Required: Must be submitted every year
Download PDF →

Home Repair & Rehabilitation

Repair and Rehab Application

Application for home repair assistance for non-HUD homes. Includes repairs to interior, exterior, and roof.

Used for: Home Repairs Assistance (Non-HUD), Home Repair Loans (Over-Income)
Download PDF →

Repair and Rehab Update

Update form for existing repair and rehabilitation applications or ongoing projects.

Download PDF →

Repair and Rehab Emergency Application

Emergency application for urgent home repairs that cannot wait for the standard application process.

Download PDF →

Rental Assistance Programs

First and Last Month's Rent Application

Application for assistance with first and last month's rent to help with initial occupancy costs.

Download PDF →

Student Rental Assistance Application

Rental assistance for full-time students attending an accredited college or university.

Eligibility: Full-time enrollment at accredited institution required
Download PDF →
